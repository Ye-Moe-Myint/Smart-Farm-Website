About Us

We are a part of 5G project in Chulalongkorn University having collaboration with AIS.

In the 5G projects, there are many kinds of IoT projects which can be enhanced by the effecient use of 5G Technology. We are focusing on the smart agricultural projects which can help the farms to make easier their work of monitoring and improve the yield by using the analysis and optimization on the historical data from the sensors of the system.
