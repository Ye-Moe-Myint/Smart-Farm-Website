Taking Survey at Nan Province, Thailand

At 29th July 2019, we went to Nan Province to do survey.

  • 29 July 2019
  • Ye Moe Myint
  • No Comments

There were about 7 places we travel to take surveys. The purpose of the trip was to understand the real world conditions of the farms and fields and to imporve the working processes and yield of the field. We found out that one of the main problems of the farms is Water Resources. We build some kinds of dan to stored water and transfer them to specific farms using hydraulic mechanism without the help of motors to minimize the use of Power.
